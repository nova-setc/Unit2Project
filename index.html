What makes a robot?

When we think of the word "robot", we often think of what might more accurately be referred to as an android (a robot resembling a human, often designed to assist humans in daily activities).

In reality, any machine which performs automated tasks can be considered a robot.

Robots are not just science-fiction

While robots may bring to mind the stuff of novels, movies, or television, the first robot was actually invented around 420 BCE. That's well over 2000 years ago!

There are even robots present in many houses today! Though automated vacuums can seem perfectly ordinary to us now, only a few decades ago, such a thing would have seemed a distant future.

While personal assistance androids seen in popular media such as the video game "Detroit: Become Human" do not exist in our current time, the future may be closer than we think.
