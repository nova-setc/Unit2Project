~420 BCE

The first known robot is created: a steam-powered bird, capable of flight.

~200 BCE

Some of the earliest known references to attempted creations of automata (human-shaped robots).

Before 100 CE

Scholars of Alexandria publish schematics detailing proposed methods for the creation a variety of robotic inventions.

1495

Leonardo da Vinci creates a design for a humanoid robot.

1560s

Gianello della Torre constructs an automaton able to imitate walking. The robot is also able to move its eyes, lips, and head.

1738

Jacques de Vaucanson builds a robotic duck, able to perform several functions to imitate living ducks.

1898

Nikola Tesla publicly demonstrates the first radio-controlled craft: a boat.
